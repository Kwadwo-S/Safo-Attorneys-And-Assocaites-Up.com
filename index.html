about us

Safo attorneys and associates is a profound company in Ghana that provide expert legal advice and procurement consulting services to individuals and businesses of all sizes.
We have executed unsurpassed services, unmatched skills and unprecedented quality through an innovative management team headed by our visionary, creative, goal driven,young and energetic CEO, Honourable Lawyer Adwoa Safo.

Our team of highly skilled legal practitioners and procurement consultants are committed to ensuring successful resolution of all your legal and procurement concerns.
From contracts to negotiations, we provide personalized and comprehensive legal solutions and procurement consultancy tailored to your unique needs.
We have experienced and professional staffs who understands the complex legal and business landscape and will work with you to provide the best advice and support to achieve your goals.
Whether it's contractual disputes, commercial law, procurement issues, or any other legal matter, our team is here to help you make informed decisions that protect your interests and ensure success. We select from a wide range of options the most viable solution to each new challenge. Our dedication to this principle motivates us to produce consistent results that exceeds the expectation of clients.

With over ten years of experience and in-depth knowledge of the law, you can rest assured that your case is in capable hands.
Our reputation speaks for itself, with a track record of successful cases across a wide range of industries.
Our goal is to not only provide exceptional legal and procurement services, but also build lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, respect, and results.
